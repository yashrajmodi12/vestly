About & disclaimer

What Vestly is — and isn't

A clear, honest explanation of how this tool works, where its data comes from, and what it can't do for you.

🎯What Vestly is

Vestly is a free educational tool that helps you think through what a diversified stock and ETF portfolio could look like for a given budget, time horizon, and risk preference. It pulls live prices where possible, simulates historical performance patterns, and shows you the math behind different strategies — all in plain language.

It's meant to be a starting point for learning, not a final answer.

🚫What Vestly is not

Vestly is not a licensed financial advisor, registered investment advisor (RIA), or broker-dealer. Nothing on this site is personalized financial, investment, tax, or legal advice.
  • It does not know your full financial picture — debt, emergency savings, insurance needs, or tax situation.
  • It does not account for fees your specific broker may charge.
  • It cannot predict the future. Every projection shown is illustrative math, not a forecast.
  • The "picks" suggested are a curated, simplified list — not a recommendation that these are the best or only options for you.

Before making real investment decisions, especially with significant money, consider talking to a licensed financial advisor who can look at your complete situation.

⚠️Investing risk, in plain terms

All investing involves risk, including the possible loss of the money you put in. A few things worth internalizing:

  • Past performance doesn't guarantee future results. A stock that grew 20%/year for the last 5 years can still drop next year.
  • Diversification reduces risk but doesn't eliminate it. Markets can decline broadly during recessions or crises.
  • Higher potential returns come with higher potential losses. There's no strategy that's both high-growth and risk-free.
  • Only invest money you won't need soon. Money needed within 1–2 years generally shouldn't be in stocks.

🔒Your data & privacy

If you create an account, your saved plans (budget, strategy, holdings) are stored securely using Supabase and are never visible to other users or sold to third parties. Vestly doesn't run ads and doesn't share your data with advertisers.

You can delete any saved plan at any time from the "My Plans" panel.

Vestly is an independent, free project. If you spot something inaccurate or have a suggestion, feedback is always welcome — this tool is meant to keep improving.
